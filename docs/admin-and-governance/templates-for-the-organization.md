# Templates For The Organization

Templates are more than files. They are a way to standardize how work starts.

## Why It Matters

When people start from old documents, old mistakes continue. Approved templates reduce formatting issues, branding drift, missing sections, and outdated language.

## What To Standardize

Start with high-volume or high-visibility documents: proposals, reports, presentations, meeting packs, forms, and policy documents.

## Ownership Model

Every template needs an owner, review cadence, and retirement path. If nobody owns a template, it will eventually become untrusted.
