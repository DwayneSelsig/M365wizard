# Organization Assets Library

An organization assets library makes approved assets available to people where they already create Microsoft 365 content.

## Why It Matters

Templates and brand assets often spread through old files, copied folders, and private chats. That creates inconsistent branding and outdated starting points.

An organization assets library gives people a trusted source for reusable content.

## Good Candidates

- PowerPoint templates.
- Word templates.
- Excel templates.
- Logos and brand images.
- Approved document starting points.

## Governance Questions

Decide who can publish assets, who reviews updates, how assets are named, and when old versions are retired.

The library should make the approved choice easy and the outdated choice unnecessary.
