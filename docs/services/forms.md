# Forms

Microsoft Forms collects responses through surveys, quizzes, polls, and simple intake forms.

## Best For

Use Forms when people need to submit information and the response does not need to be managed by the submitter afterward.

## Be Careful With

Forms is not a full process management tool. If the response becomes a tracked record or task, connect it to Lists, Planner, or another workflow.
