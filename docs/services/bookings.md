# Bookings

Bookings lets people schedule appointments from available times you define.

## Best For

Use Bookings for office hours, consultations, intake meetings, service appointments, and training sessions.

## Be Careful With

Do not use Bookings when a normal calendar invite is enough. It shines when scheduling is repeatable and should feel like a managed service.
