# Teams

Microsoft Teams is a shared workspace for active collaboration.

## Best For

Use Teams for projects, departments, working groups, recurring meetings, shared files, and task coordination.

## Be Careful With

Do not create a Team for every announcement, community, or published page. Teams works best when members actively work together.
