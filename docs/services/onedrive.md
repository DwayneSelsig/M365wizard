# OneDrive

OneDrive for Business is your personal work file space in Microsoft 365.

## Best For

Use OneDrive for drafts, personal work documents, notes, and short-term sharing with specific people.

## Be Careful With

Do not let team-owned work live in one person's OneDrive for too long. Move shared work to Teams or SharePoint when it becomes part of a team process.
