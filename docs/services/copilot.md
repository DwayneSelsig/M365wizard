# Copilot

Microsoft 365 Copilot helps people work with content, context, and prompts across Microsoft 365.

## Best For

Use Copilot to summarize, draft, compare, brainstorm, transform content, and find patterns across work you already have access to.

## Be Careful With

Copilot reflects your permissions and your content quality. Poor file ownership, unclear permissions, and outdated documents will reduce the value of AI assistance.

## Data Protection And Compliance

Microsoft Purview can add classification, sensitivity labels, DLP, Audit, Communication Compliance, eDiscovery, retention, and data-security posture capabilities to supported Copilots, agents, and other AI apps. Coverage and configuration differ by app and agent, and some scenarios require additional licensing or pay-as-you-go billing. Verify the exact experience in Microsoft's [current Purview guidance for generative AI](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/purview/ai-microsoft-purview).

Purview supplements the foundation; it does not repair excessive permissions, unclear ownership, or obsolete source content.
