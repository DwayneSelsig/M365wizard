# Excel

Excel is the Microsoft 365 app for spreadsheets, analysis, models, and structured calculations.

## Best For

Use Excel for analysis, lightweight models, calculations, tabular planning, and reports that need formulas or pivots.

## Be Careful With

Excel is not always the right long-term database. If multiple people track operational records over time, consider Lists, Dataverse, or a governed system.
