# Planner

Planner helps teams track lightweight work with tasks, buckets, assignments, due dates, and progress.

## Best For

Use Planner for team task boards, small project plans, checklists, campaign work, and recurring operational tasks.

## Be Careful With

Planner is not designed for complex project management. Keep it focused on work the team can understand at a glance.
