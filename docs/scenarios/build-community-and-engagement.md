# Build Community And Engagement

Community work needs visibility, participation, and a reason for people to return.

## Recommended Pattern

Use Viva Engage for broad communities, leadership communication, questions, knowledge sharing, and employee engagement. Use Teams when a smaller group needs to coordinate work.

## Good Community Signals

- People from different departments should participate.
- Questions should be visible and reusable.
- Leaders want a conversational channel.
- The topic is ongoing, not a short project.

## Keep It Healthy

Communities need hosts. Define who welcomes people, answers questions, highlights useful posts, and keeps the space alive.
