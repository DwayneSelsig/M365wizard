# Schedule Appointments

Appointments are easier when people can choose from controlled availability instead of sending messages back and forth.

## Recommended Pattern

Use Bookings when external or internal people need to schedule time with a service, department, or role. Use Outlook when the appointment is personal or one-off.

## Use Bookings For

- Office hours.
- Intake meetings.
- Consultations.
- Training sessions.
- Service desk appointments.

## Why It Matters

Bookings turns availability into a managed service. It reduces manual scheduling and makes the appointment process feel consistent for the person booking time.
